PERSONAL INTRESSES

I am Jeffrey Waegneer, the founder of Journey Software Solutions. After working in the software industry for several years, I made the decision to establish my own company, Journey Software Solutions. My passion for software development led me to freelance work, with my primary focus being on developing solutions for backend systems.

Within software development, my main interest lies in backend systems. Front-end development has never been my preference. In my free time, I enjoy delving into artificial intelligence. It's fascinating to witness the tremendous progress this field has made in recent years. Personally, I prefer focusing on more intricate algorithms. I have programmed various genetic algorithms and trained them to play challenging games.

Aside from software development, traveling—alone or with friends—is a great passion of mine, which also inspired the name of the company. The world is so vast that not exploring it would be a loss for me. Additionally, I'm a social person who enjoys gathering with colleagues or friends for meals or drinks.
